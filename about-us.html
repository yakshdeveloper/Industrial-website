About Us

27 Years of Expertise in Manufactring of
Screw Barrel for Plastic Processing Industry

Tirupati Industries has over 27 years of experience producing Screw Barrel, Groove Sleeve, die head, Sizer, Sleeve Fitted Front Barrel, Candle filter assembly, and all types of plastic extrusion machine parts. Tirupati Industries is the leading Screw Barrel Manufacturer not only because of our superior quality but also because of our ethical business practices. Our products are currently exported to Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, the United Arab Emirates, Sri Lanka, Poland, and other countries. Apart from manufacturing we also supply spares for PVC, HDPE, and Blown film plants.

We are a Sole Proprietorship company based in Ahmedabad (Gujarat, India) incorporated in the year 1998. We work hard to provide our valued customers with a high-quality collection of plastic machinery parts that meet international standards.

Birth of the Tiurpati Industries in the field of Plastic Industries.

In 1994, Mr. Kamlesh C. Patel, at 29 years old, left his job and Tirupati Industries was born in Ahmedabad, India. Back then, it was just a small factory on rent, where Mr. Patel started with 1 Lathe and 1 Milling machine. The first product was a set of single screw barrel for the pipe extruder fitted with a groove feed. That first screw barrel set became the prime reason for the present growth. The tag of Best Screw Barrel Manufacturer in Ahmedabad was given by our beloved customers, who trusted our quality in the beginning and gave us the chance.

We established our presence in the field of Screw Barrel.

In just 6 years, Tirupati Industries had established its presence in the screw barrel manufacturing industry. By the end of the year 2000, Tirupati Industries was having its land of 2000 sq ft and more than 6 machines. Under the guidance of Mr. Patel, We started manufacturing Counter-rotating Twin Screw Barrel. We were also providing the Bimetallic Twin Screw Barrel with TC coating and that’s what made us the Bimetallic Screw Barrel Manufacturer. In just 6 years we had more than 300 customers.

16 years of efficient work, expansion of products, and export to the world.

By the end of the year 2010, Tirupati Industries had expanded its production area to over 6000 sq ft. At this point we knew that we are good at manufacturing screw barrels, so we procured all the necessary machines to expand our in-house manufacturing capabilities. From the beginning, the quality of our screw barrel was our main priority, and due to that reason, we found our growth in exporting our screw barrel to other countries. We have exported to more than 10 countries by 2010. We open a research and development department to increase the quality standards of our screw barrel.

Tirupati Industries marks its position as a Quality Screw Barrel Manufacturer

Tirupati Industries had never stopped growing. In 20 years, we had a great experience in the screw barrel manufacturing business. In the year 2015, we had more than 900 customers and we had exported more than 500 sets of screw barrel to the world. By this point, we had 18 precision manufacturing machines. Due to the hard work of our engineers, we customized the designs of our screw barrel to produce even more throughput with a longer life span. We also started implementing lean manufacturing for better production efficiency and improvised quality standards.

It’s a longe path and we only reached few milestones, more is yet to come.

At the present Tirupati Industries has increased its production area above 10,000 sq ft. We have more than 20 machines. The Nitriding furnace facility and the CNC deep hole machine are also on the planning list. Our customer list has crossed the mark of 1500 and we make sure we do not lose a single customer over the quality of our products and service. Tirupati Industries has collaborated with Wintech Industries, who are a pioneer in manufacturing all types of extrusion plants. Due to our lean manufacturing implementation and higher quality standards, we have confidence in our products for a better run.
