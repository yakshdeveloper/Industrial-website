Die

Extrusion plant die requires high accuracy and precision to deliver the qualitative output. At Tirupati industries, we manufacture mainly two types of extrusion plant die for HDPE and RPVC. Each die is made with state-of-the-art machine tools to ensure that you get the best quality pipe. With our team of experts we take care of each dimension tolerance with efficient quality control in each production step.

RPVC Extrusion Plant Die

At Tirupati Industries, We Manufacturer PVC extrusion die up to 315 mm Pipe size For PVC Pipe Extrusion Plant.

Design: Spider design, which balances the melt ﬂow by providing a more uniform exit velocity across the entire die exit.

Features:

  • High volume low pressure design ensures vast thickness range
  • Uniform melt distribution throughout the entire pipe circumference
  • Twin die heads for higher outputs in smaller diameters
  • Extruders utilization from 80-100% even with smaller pipes
  • Elimination of continuous spider marks (ID to OD) allowing better mechanical properties of the pipe
  • Ideal residence time profile with selective temperature control on the sensitive material
