Without a durable and precise barrel, there is no use of any extruder screw. At Tirupati industries, we manufacture all kinds of barrel to
support all kinds of extrusion screw. We recommend to take our barrel with our screw for better performance as they are made with
greater precision and accuracy.

Single Barrel

At Tirupati industries, for each type of single extrusion screw you can get the single extrusion barrel with higher hardness, strong wear resistant and corrosion resistant. We also provide custom design suitable to your extrusion screw.

We manufacture the single barrel with internal diameter (ID) ranging from 20mm to 180mm and length up to 6000mm.

Warranty: We give warranty of 1 to 1.5 year in our Single Barrel

Material: EN41B MUSCO procured from authorized dealer

Nittriding Hardness: 66 to 68 HRC

  • Accurate Compatibility with Die and Gearbox

Sleeve Fitted Front Barrel

At Tirupati industries, Each Twin Barrel is fitted with Sleeve Fitted Front Barrel to increase the life of Screw Barrel. In Screws, the Metering section goes under heavy wear. The Screws are protected with Bimetallic coating and in order to protect the Barrel, It is fitted with a Front Barrel with HSS M2 Grade sleeve in it.

Features

  • Certified HSS M2 Material
  • Precise fitting with Twin Barrel
  • No trace of any steps in assembled barrel ID bores
  • No sticking and burning of material
