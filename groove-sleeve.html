Groove Sleeve

At Tirupati Industries, we provide Groove Sleeve/Groove Feed for our single barrel. Grove Feed ensures better melting due to increase shear and higher output capacity. We manufacture Groove Feed with diameter ranging from 37mm to 150mm.

Material: EN41B and D2. (Certified)

Features:


  • Seamless connection
  • Hardchrome plated
  • Long lasting and corrosion resistance plating
  • Wear and corrosion resistance
